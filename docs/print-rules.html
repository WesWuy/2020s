2020s: The Board Game

Prototype v0.2 Rules Quickstart

Survive the decade without losing your mind, your money, or your grip on reality.

Objective

Race from 2020 to 2030. The first player to reach the final space with at least 1 Sanity, 1 Money, and 1 Influence wins.

Components

  • 1 board track with 40 spaces
  • 6 character cards
  • 1 six-sided die
  • 60 main cards: Global Chaos, Media Meltdown, Hidden Hand, Political Flip
  • 5 optional Final Reckoning cards
  • Stat counters for Sanity, Money, and Influence
  • 1 marker for Red Cycle / Blue Cycle

Setup

  1. Each player chooses one character.
  2. Set each player’s Sanity, Money, and Influence to match their character card.
  3. Shuffle each deck separately.
  4. Choose Red Cycle or Blue Cycle randomly.
  5. All players start on Space 1: 2020: Everything Is Fine.

Turn Order

  1. Roll one die.
  2. Move forward that many spaces.
  3. Resolve the board space.
  4. Draw and resolve a card if required.
  5. Apply any active effects.
  6. Check whether a stat hit zero.

Core Stats

  • Sanity: emotional resilience and resistance to doomscrolling.
  • Money: economic survival, assets, side hustles, and emergency supplies.
  • Influence: clout, followers, credibility, institutional power, or narrative control.

When a Stat Hits Zero

  • Sanity = 0: You enter Doomscroll Mode. Skip your next turn unless another player gives you 1 Influence.
  • Money = 0: You are Broke. Move backward 2 spaces and gain 1 Sanity because expectations are now lower.
  • Influence = 0: You are Shadowbanned. You may not gain Influence until your next turn.

Board Cycle

The board is always in Red Cycle or Blue Cycle. Political Flip cards can change the cycle. Some cards behave differently depending on the active cycle.

  • Red Cycle: populism, deregulation, market mania, culture war, anti-institutional energy.
  • Blue Cycle: institutional control, safety policy, climate urgency, censorship accusations, bureaucracy.

Winning

Reach Space 40: 2030: You Made It? with at least 1 Sanity, 1 Money, and 1 Influence.

If you reach 2030 without the required stats, draw a Final Reckoning card.
