How to Play

You are trying to make it from 2020 to 2030 without falling apart. Every survivor juggles three stats:

  • Sanity — what is left of your peace of mind.
  • Money — your buffer against the chaos.
  • Influence — how loudly the timeline listens to you.

Pick a way to play:

🎮 Video Game Mode

Choose a Dystopia Run or Utopia Run and a character, then make one big choice each year. Keep your stats above zero and watch the Timeline Health meter — your ending is a shareable broadcast report.

🎲 Board Game Mode

A digital assistant for the 40-space tabletop game: roll, move, and draw from roughly 110 satirical cards on your way to 2030. Print the kit below to play at a real table.
