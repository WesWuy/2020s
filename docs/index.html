Design Note

This is a satirical, text-first prototype. The goal is to test the game loop before investing in polished art, Kickstarter assets, manufacturing files, or final lore.

Core Pitch

2020s: The Board Game is a chaotic satirical race from 2020 to 2030. Players survive pandemics, market shocks, media meltdowns, political flips, billionaire schemes, tech panic, culture wars, and reality itself becoming negotiable.

First player to reach 2030 with at least 1 Sanity, 1 Money, and 1 Influence wins.

Core Stats

  • Sanity: emotional resilience and resistance to doomscrolling.
  • Money: cash, assets, canned goods, and general economic survival.
  • Influence: clout, institutional power, followers, or narrative control.

Turn Order

  1. Roll one die.
  2. Move forward that many spaces.
  3. Resolve the board space.
  4. Draw a card if required.
  5. Apply active effects.
  6. Check if a stat hit zero.

When a Stat Hits Zero

Sanity = 0

You enter Doomscroll Mode. Skip your next turn unless another player gives you 1 Influence.

Money = 0

You are Broke. Move backward 2 spaces and gain 1 Sanity because expectations are now lower.

Influence = 0

You are Shadowbanned. You may not gain Influence until your next turn.

Board Cycle

The board is always in either Red Cycle or Blue Cycle. Political Flip cards can change the cycle. Some cards behave differently depending on the active cycle.

Red Cycle

Populism, deregulation, market mania, culture war, anti-institutional energy.

Blue Cycle

Institutional control, safety policy, climate urgency, censorship accusations, bureaucracy.

First Playtest Scorecard

  • Did people understand the rules within 5 minutes?
  • Did the table laugh at least 5 times?
  • Did all three stats matter?
  • Did anyone feel stuck or eliminated too early?
  • Did the political cycle mechanic change decisions?
  • Was the ending satisfying?
  • Did any card feel mean without being funny?
  • Did anyone immediately ask to play again?
