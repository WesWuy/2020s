Core Look

The visual world should feel like a live news broadcast from a broken timeline, illustrated as a comic-book apocalypse. It should be dramatic, funny, high-contrast, readable, and meme-able.

  • Retro-futurist broadcast graphics.
  • Comic-book halftone texture.
  • Bold lower-thirds and warning labels.
  • Glitchy Dystopia palette: red, gold, black, electric blue.
  • Hopeful Utopia palette: green, blue, gold, clean contrast.
  • Characters should look iconic, exaggerated, and immediately readable.

Reusable Prompt Formula

Use this formula when generating final art assets:

Satirical retro-futurist news broadcast meets comic-book apocalypse, bold halftone shading, dramatic lower-third graphics, neon broadcast lighting, cinematic composition, exaggerated but not grotesque character design, high contrast, readable silhouettes, dark humor, 16:9 game art panel.
