# 2020s: The Boardgame — Quickstart v0.16

## Goal

Reach **2030** before everyone else while keeping all four stats above zero.

Stats:

- Money
- Sanity
- Freedom
- Influence

## Setup

1. Pick a character.
2. Start on the first 2020 space.
3. Set stats to Money 5, Sanity 5, Freedom 5, Influence 3, then apply your character modifiers.
4. Set Panic, Control, and Market meters to 0.
5. Deal each player one Survival card.
6. Choose starting mode: Red Cycle or Blue Cycle.

## Your Turn

1. Roll the die.
2. Move forward.
3. Resolve the space.
4. Draw a card if told to.
5. Make one choice if the card gives you options.
6. Check chaos meters.

## Chaos Meters

When a meter hits 6, it collapses:

- Panic collapse: everyone loses 1 Sanity
- Control collapse: everyone loses 1 Freedom
- Market collapse: everyone loses 1 Money

Then reset that meter to 0.

## NPC Mode

If any stat hits 0:

- Miss your next turn.
- Reset that stat to 2.
- Keep playing.

No one is eliminated.

## Red Cycle / Blue Cycle

Election spaces and some cards use the current political cycle.

- Red Cycle tends to boost Money/Freedom and raise Panic.
- Blue Cycle tends to boost Influence/Stability and raise Control.

The joke is the mirror: every side thinks it is saving the world, and every side causes chaos.

## Win

First player to reach 2030 with at least 1 Money, 1 Sanity, 1 Freedom, and 1 Influence wins.

## Table Rule

Keep choices fast. If someone takes too long, the table may yell:

**TIMELINE PRESSURE!**
