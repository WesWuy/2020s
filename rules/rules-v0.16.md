# 2020s: The Boardgame — Rules v0.16

## Game Summary

Race from 2020 to 2030 while surviving social, economic, political, technological, and conspiracy-fueled chaos.

Players win by reaching 2030 with at least 1 point remaining in each survival stat.

## Players

2–6 players

## Target Time

20–45 minutes

## Components

- 1 timeline board
- 2–6 player tokens
- 1 six-sided die
- 4 stat trackers per player
- 3 global chaos meters
- Character cards
- Headline cards
- Conspiracy cards
- Survival cards
- Scandal cards

## Player Stats

Each player tracks:

| Stat | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Money | Resources and financial stability |
| Sanity | Mental and emotional stability |
| Freedom | Ability to move, act, and choose |
| Influence | Social power, platform reach, credibility |

Recommended starting stats:

| Stat | Starting Value |
|---|---:|
| Money | 5 |
| Sanity | 5 |
| Freedom | 5 |
| Influence | 3 |

Character archetypes may modify these starting values.

## Global Chaos Meters

The table shares three global meters:

| Meter | What it represents | Collapse effect at 6 |
|---|---|---|
| Panic | fear, confusion, doomscrolling | Everyone loses 1 Sanity |
| Control | restrictions, surveillance, institutional pressure | Everyone loses 1 Freedom |
| Market | inflation, crashes, shortages, speculation | Everyone loses 1 Money |

Each meter starts at 0.

When a meter reaches 6:

1. Resolve the collapse effect.
2. Reset that meter to 0.
3. Add one collapse mark to the final timeline report.

## Setup

1. Place all player tokens on the first 2020 space.
2. Set Panic, Control, and Market to 0.
3. Each player chooses or is dealt one character archetype.
4. Each player sets starting stats.
5. Shuffle all card decks.
6. Deal one Survival card to each player.
7. Choose Red Cycle or Blue Cycle as the starting political mode.

## Turn Order

On your turn:

1. Roll one six-sided die.
2. Move forward that many spaces.
3. Resolve the space you land on.
4. Draw a card if the space requires it.
5. Apply your character power if relevant.
6. Check chaos meter collapse.
7. End turn.

## Board Spaces

| Space Type | Effect |
|---|---|
| Timeline | Resolve the written space effect |
| Breaking News | Draw one Headline card |
| Square & Compass | Draw one Conspiracy card |
| Survival Check | Draw one Survival card or recover 1 stat |
| Scandal | Draw one Scandal card |
| Market Crash | Lose Money or take the risky recovery option |
| Pandemic Panic | Lose Sanity or Freedom |
| Election Year | Apply Red Cycle or Blue Cycle effect |
| Tech Leap | Gain Influence or move forward |
| Awakening | Recover Sanity or Freedom |

## Card Types

### Headline Cards

Public events that usually affect one or more players and may move chaos meters.

### Conspiracy Cards

Absurd chance-style cards triggered by Square & Compass spaces. These are high-chaos, high-comedy cards.

### Survival Cards

Helpful cards. Most are held until used.

### Scandal Cards

Reputation, media, and institutional chaos. These often affect Influence and Control.

## Choices

Many cards offer a choice. The active player must choose one option unless the card says everyone chooses.

Choices are meant to create funny tradeoffs, not long negotiation.

Recommended table rule:

> If a player takes more than 20 seconds to choose, the table may begin chanting “timeline pressure.”

## NPC Mode

If any player stat reaches 0, that player enters NPC Mode.

NPC Mode:

1. The player misses their next turn.
2. The stat that reached 0 resets to 2.
3. Other stats remain unchanged.
4. The player returns on their following turn.

Players are never eliminated.

## Red Cycle / Blue Cycle

The game can run in two mirrored political modes.

### Red Cycle

Red Cycle generally rewards Freedom and Money but tends to increase Panic and media chaos.

### Blue Cycle

Blue Cycle generally rewards Influence and institutional stability but tends to increase Control and social pressure.

The mode can switch when:

- An Election Year space says so
- A card forces a cycle switch
- The table chooses chaos mode before the game

## Character Archetypes

Characters create asymmetry and replayability.

Recommended starting archetypes:

- The Prepper
- The Influencer
- The Crypto Bro
- The Remote Worker
- The Activist
- The Normie

See `data/prototype/characters-v0.16.csv`.

## Winning

The first player to reach 2030 with at least 1 Money, 1 Sanity, 1 Freedom, and 1 Influence wins.

## Special Endings

Optional flavor endings:

| Ending | Trigger |
|---|---|
| Survivor Ending | Reach 2030 first |
| Awakened Ending | Reach 2030 with Sanity and Freedom both 5+ |
| Influencer Ending | Reach 2030 with Influence 7+ |
| Bunker Ending | Reach 2030 with Money 7+ but Influence 1 or less |
| Great Reset Ending | Control collapses twice before anyone reaches 2030 |
| Doomscroll Ending | Panic collapses twice before anyone reaches 2030 |
| Black Swan Ending | Market collapses twice before anyone reaches 2030 |
